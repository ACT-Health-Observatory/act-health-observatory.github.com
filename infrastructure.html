CARDAT infrastructure

The online data storage and analysis platform has been designed for use of CAR members and their nominated affiliated.

There are three aspects of this system:

  • a cloud based data storage system (CloudStor)
  • a catalogue of available datasets (CAR Data Inventory)
  • an online data analysis platform (CoESRA)
