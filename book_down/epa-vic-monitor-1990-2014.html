17.2 EPA_Vic_monitor_1990_2014

shortname EPA_Vic_monitor_1990_2014
title EPA_Vic_monitor_1990_2014
creator EPA Victoria
contact_email ivan.hanigan@sydney.edu.au
abstract Air quality averages data from 1990-2014 for all EPA monitoring sites in Victoria. Measurements of one or more of the following are taken: 1-hourly averages for PM10 (TEOM), PM2.5 (BAM), CO, NO2, O3, SO2, Visibility reduction; 24-hourly averages for PM2.5 (Partisol) and Lead. See Data Info Panel tab in .xlsx of data for more information. Downloaded from https://www.data.vic.gov.au/data/dataset/epa-air-watch-all-sites-air-quality-hourly-averages-yearly | | Annual averages for CO, NO2, O3, SO2, PM2.5, PM10 derived where there were observations for >75% of hours each day and that those days totalled >75% of all days each year.
